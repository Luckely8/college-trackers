Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our student services.

What makes your services different for Amazon business families?

We specialize in helping students leverage their unique background in a family business. We know which colleges value entrepreneurial experience, how to talk about business involvement in essays, and specific scholarships available to children of business owners.

When should I start working with you?

Ideal timing is spring of junior year through fall of senior year. However, we offer services for sophomores (early planning) and even seniors who need last-minute help. Earlier planning always yields better results!

Do you help with financial aid and scholarships?

Yes! Our Amazon Legacy package includes scholarship research specific to business families. We also provide guidance on how to complete financial aid forms and what to expect as a student from a business-owning family.

Can you help if I'm interested in business majors?

Absolutely! We have specialized knowledge of top business undergraduate programs and what they look for in applicants. We can help you build a compelling narrative around your family business experience.
